Assumptions — Model benchmarks, multipliers & methodology

Current calculator (left): Effective TPM = RPM × (Input + Output). GPU = ⌈ Effective TPM ÷ TPM/GPU ⌉.
Weighted calculator (right): Effective TPM = RPM × (Input + Multiplier × Output). GPU = ⌈ Effective TPM ÷ TPM/GPU ⌉.
